73 Caprice


This vehicle underwent an exterior restoration and high performance
modifications. Vehicle has a total of 824 physical labor hours; 541 where in body & paint; 283 where in fabrication. Vehicle build time 7 months.

Finishes:

Body all painted apart in Sherwin Williams Ultra 7000 line; Phantom Grey with
Re-Stacked clear. Engine painted in Blood Orange pearl. TT Piping
finished in Cerakote “V” Series Cobra Black.

Drive Train:

Texas Speed LS3 crate engine with a 4L80E Transmission. Custom Built in-house twin turbo set up with mirror image Precision Turbos and
manifolds. CX Racing Twin Intercoolers.

Vehicle Management:

Fuel Tech FT600 EFI System which provides full vehicle
control; including but not limited to Traction Control, Engine & Transmission function, all Cooling, Stability Control & Safety features for self-management.

Frame:

Original Frame has been notched 1½” for rear wheel fitment. Painted in Jet black.

Suspension:

Suspension: Custom in house built Adjustable 4 link. Chevy 12 Bolt rear end notched 4” on each side to fit wheel backspacing. Racing Axels, Coilovers and 4.11
Gear.

Wheels & Brakes:

Forgiato chrome Wheels; 26”x10” front & 26”x12” rear. Baer Brakes 6P system with vehicle matching painted Calipers. Brake Booster system with Baer
Re-Master Cylinder.

Exhaust:

Magnaflow

Interior:

Original Interior with Vintage Air Gen IV A/C System with custom lines and distribution block.
