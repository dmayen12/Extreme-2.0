1969 Chevrolet Camaro RS
This vehicle underwent a full restoration. Has a total of 1671 physical labor hours put into the build; 297 where in the paint dept.
Vehicle took two years to complete.

Specs

Body:

 

Vehicle was painted with Sherwin Williams Ultra 7000 line; Torch Red with a Carbon Fiber accent hockey stick stripe and
re-stacked clear. All trim was powder coated black. Front & Rear spoilers are pure Carbon Fiber along with the inner fenders. All body hardware is stainless steel. Rear frame rails
were replaced and moved in total a total 8 inches to allow for the wider rear wheels.
Rear Tubs measure 17 inches in width.

Drive Train:

 

Super Charged LSA crate engine with 4L80E transmission. Custom in-house built carbon fiber lid with custom super charger core cooling system. Rick’s Fuel tank with braided in-rail fuel lines..

Exhaust:

 

Custom built in-house stainless 3” oval system with Magnaflow mufflers and electrical exhaust cutouts.

Frame & Suspension:

 

 Speed tech performance front sub-frame with frame connectors. Speed tech front tubular system with custom in-house built billet spindles. Rear 4 link set up with a 9”
diamond rear end sporting a 3.70 gear ratio.

Wheels & Brakes:

 

Vellano 3 piece Carbon Fiber wheels with a machine brushed face. Front 20”x9”; rear 21”x15”. Brembo Brakes GT system; 8 piston calipers with a 15” drilled & slotted rotor. In-rail braided brake lines.

Interior:

 

Completed by Pro-Auto Custom Interiors in TN.
Vintage Air A/C system with custom in-house built hoses. Dakota Digital Dash with Carbon Fiber Housing.
